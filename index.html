ABOUT ME

I am Tharunkumar R, an M.Tech student in Embedded Systems at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bangalore, with a background in Electronics and Communication Engineering. I am passionate about building intelligent embedded systems where hardware and software work together to solve real-world problems.

My interests include microcontroller-based system design, real-time sensing, robotics, and embedded safety systems. I enjoy working with platforms such as STM32 microcontrollers and ESP32, developing solutions that integrate sensors, communication interfaces, and efficient embedded software.

Some of my key projects include a Vehicle Collision Avoidance System using sensor fusion, a Music Genre Classification system using machine learning, and an ICU Tele-Monitoring device for healthcare applications. These projects strengthened my experience in Embedded C programming, sensor interfacing, microcontroller development, and system-level design.

I am always eager to explore emerging technologies and take on challenging engineering problems. My goal is to contribute to innovative solutions in embedded intelligence, robotics, and smart sensing systems.
